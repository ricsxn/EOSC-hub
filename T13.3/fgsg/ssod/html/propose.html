This space provides the necessary information to propose your own Science Gateway interface in case the available applications are not suitable for your specific use case.

There are two possible supporting strategies, the first foresees the integration of your proposed application inside available applications of this site, the second the creation of a new Science Gateway solution specifically tailored for your requirements. For both approaches it is necessary to describe the application requirements, while in the second approach it is necessary also to establish a strategy plan for hosting the necessary FutureGateway services.

To propose your application, just contact the support staff, starting to provide a brief description of your use case.
